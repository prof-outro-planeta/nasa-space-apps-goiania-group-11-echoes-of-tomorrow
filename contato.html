Welcome to "Echoes of Tomorrow"! We are a team participating in the NASA Hackathon, passionate about educating and inspiring people to understand the complex interconnections of our planet. Our mission is to create immersive experiences that connect the audience to tourist destinations while raising awareness about the environmental challenges we face.

Utilizing NASA data, we offer a journey that not only informs but also motivates action. We believe that each of us has the power to make a difference and shape a more sustainable future. Join us on this adventure and discover how your choices can impact the world!
